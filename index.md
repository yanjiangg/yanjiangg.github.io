I am a postdoctoral scholar at the Stanford Center on Early Childhood. My research examines early care and education systems, with a focus on workforce equity, educator well-being, and how quality is defined, measured, and studied across contexts. I use computational social science, mixed methods, and text analysis to study large-scale survey, publication, and qualitative data.

## Research Interests

Early care and education  
ECE workforce equity and well-being  
Computational social science  
Text analysis and mixed methods  
Global and historical patterns in education research
