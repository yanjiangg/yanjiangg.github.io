# Research

My research focuses on early care and education systems, workforce equity, educator well-being, and computational approaches to studying education research and open-ended survey data.

## Research Areas

### Early Care and Education Workforce Equity

I study how early educators experience personal and professional challenges, and how these experiences relate to well-being, intentions to leave, and pathways for systemic change.

### ECE Quality Across Contexts

I examine how early care and education quality is defined, measured, and enacted across policy, research, and practice contexts.

### Computational Social Science and Text Analysis

I use computational text analysis, topic modeling, embeddings, and mixed methods to study large-scale survey responses and publication data.
